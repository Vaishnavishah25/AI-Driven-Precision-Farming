Introduction to Precision Farming

Precision farming involves using advanced technologies to monitor and manage agricultural practices with high precision. This approach helps farmers make data-driven decisions to maximize yield and minimize waste.

How AI Enhances Farming

AI technologies, such as machine learning, drones, and IoT sensors, play a crucial role in precision farming. These technologies enable real-time monitoring, predictive analytics, and automated decision-making, leading to improved efficiency and productivity.
